Execution Notes

  • Intensity: working sets to 1-2 reps in reserve. Add reps or weight when you hit the top of the range with clean form.
  • On the cut: maintain loads/reps rather than chasing PRs. Maintaining performance in a deficit = retaining muscle. Protein 180-200g is what makes it work.
  • Track every session (weight × reps). Progression is invisible without it.
  • Deload every 6-8 weeks (cut volume ~40% for a week), or sooner if joints/sleep signal it.
  • Pain is a signal — if knees/ankle flare, scale back or swap, don't push through.

Core / Cardio

Core

2-3x/week, slotted into Legs & Lower days. Full circuit each session, 2-3 sets each. Don't grind to failure; control the eccentric.

Pallof press (anti-rotation)10-12 / side
Hanging leg raises10-15
Decline sit-ups (swap to cable crunch if felt in lower back)12-20
Optional: suitcase carry~30-40 sec / side

Cardio

30-min incline walk after every lift (5×/week). Incline ~8-12%, nose-breathing pace.
Optional: one easy 30-40 min walk/run on a non-lift day if you want more.
